---User Instructions---

This is a multiple choice question and answer test.

There are no scores being kept...so, you might think of it as a 'drill'; feel free to answer the same question/s as many times as you like.


Each question contains a list of 4 possible answers that the user can select from using their mouse...; just click on whatever you believe to be the 'correct' answer to select.

If you find you've accidentally made a 'wrong' choice...; and, now, wish to change your mind; then, just simply click to choose a different answer, instead.

The answer you've chosen will be displayed as color: 'silver'.


When you've made a final guess; and, do no longer wish to change your mind, anymore; clicking the button: [Show answer] will show exactly what the 'correct' answer is.

The correct answer will be displayed as colour: green.


Click the link: [Next] to move onto answering a new question; you may continue clicking [Next] until when the end of the test is reached.

Good luck! ;-)
